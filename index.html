Our Mission

NSBE's mission is to increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.

USF Chapter

Our USF chapter actively upholds this mission through a variety of engaging programs and initiatives. We focus on fostering academic excellence, offering invaluable professional development opportunities, and implementing robust pre-college initiatives that inspire the next generation of STEM leaders. From technical workshops and career guidance to community outreach and leadership development, we strive to create a supportive and empowering environment for our members to thrive. Join us as we work to make a striking impact on both the engineering world and the broader community.
